The first convening of the Michigan Independent Citizens Redistricting Commission was held Thursday, September 17 through Friday, September 18 from 9am-5:30pm. The meeting livestreamed.

The Department of State distributed an agenda and orientation materials to the Commissioners in advance of the meeting.
